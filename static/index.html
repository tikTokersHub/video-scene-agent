Capabilities

Built for analysts who need answers they can defend.

/01

Frame-level Retrieval

Find the exact moments that matter with frame-accurate evidence retrieval across the entire video.

/02

Video-specific Rules

Automatically induce normal and abnormal rules tailored to the content of each uploaded video.

/03

Structured Answers

Clear, concise answers with rule checks and confidence scores you can trust and audit.

/04

Evidence Gallery

Review the most relevant frames side-by-side to validate every answer with visual proof.

Workflow

From raw footage to defensible answer in four steps.

1

Upload Video

Drop in surveillance footage and the pipeline extracts key frames at a fixed sample rate.

2

Generate Rules

A VLM captions frames; an LLM induces normal and abnormal rules from the scene.

3

Ask Questions

Query the video in natural language — including temporal questions spanning many frames.

4

Review Evidence

Get answers with retrieved frames and confidence scores to verify every conclusion.
